FEATURE 3: ADU Regulation Knowledge Management System

Problem Statement: California State-level and City-level ADU laws are constantly evolving with new legislation and revisions. Planners are finding it challenging to keep pace with the most current regulations, sometimes leading to inaccurate guidance…. Manual tracking of regulation changes is time-intensive and error-prone. Once a year thing.

Solution Requirements:

3.1 Dynamic Regulation Tracking

· Input: Automated monitoring of state ADU law updates and revisions

· Process: AI identifies changes in regulations and updates knowledge base

· Output: Current, accurate ADU requirement responses with version tracking

3.2 Common ADU Query Handling

· Height Questions: "Can I have a 2-story ADU?" (law changes periodically)

· Design Requirements: "What's the maximum height for garage doors?"

· Setback Rules: "What are current ADU setback requirements?"

· Compliance Checks: "Does this ADU design meet current state requirements?"

3.3 Change Alert System

· Monitoring: Track California Housing and Community Development (HCD) updates

· Notifications: Alert planning staff when regulations change

· Impact Analysis: Identify how changes affect pending applications

· Update Integration: Seamlessly incorporate new rules into AI responses

3.4 Version Control & Audit Trail

· Historical Tracking: Maintain record of regulation changes over time

· Decision Documentation: Show which version of law was applied to each decision

· Compliance Verification: Ensure responses reflect most current requirements

· Legal Protection: Provide audit trail for regulatory compliance
